Ceviche Recipe

Fresh and delicious Ceviche

Ingredients

  • Cooked Skinless Shrimp
  • Jalapenos
  • Cilantro
  • Onions
  • Tomato Juice
  • Limes Juice

Directions

  1. Put Cooked shrimp in water to thaw out.
  2. Cut up Onions,Cilantro,Tomatoes and Jalapenos.
  3. Once Shrimp is cooked, dice it all up into small pieces and add to a bowl with all cut up veggies
  4. Add a cup of tomato juice and 2 cups of lime juice and let it sit in the fridge for 2 hours.
  5. Enjoy!

Cheese Quesadillas

Cheesy and delicious quesadilla

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • choice of cheese
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Heat up pan at medium high heat.
  2. Place flour tortilla on pan and place cheese on top.
  3. Put butter on the outside of the tortilla and place back on the pan.
  4. Serve on a plate and enjoy!
